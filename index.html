Elvis Presley

Born: January 8, 1935
Education: High School Diploma
Primarily worked for: RCA Records

Biography

Elvis Aaron Presley, mostly recognized by his first name, was a singer and actor of the 20th century. In his early teens, Presley's parents would move the family to Memphis, Tennessee. It was here where Elvis' love for music grew as he constantly observed all sorts of sounds and rhythms around him, mostly notebly in the Church (gospel). While he couldn't read or write music and had no formal lessons, Elvis was a natural musican who did everything by ear.He had the ability to hear a song, pick up an instrument, and play. Elvis played the guitar, bass, and piano. Would make his first break in 1954 with his song "Hound Dog". Would later be signed into RCA records and become a national star. After some time, Elvis enlisted in the army,all while producing movies (31 to be exact). Once he came back from his 2 year serve, he would go on contstant tours until his death in 1977. Was given the title "King of Rock & Roll" for being one of the most influencial musicians of his time.
