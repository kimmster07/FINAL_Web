Johnny Cash

Born: February 26, 1935
Education: High School Diploma
Primarily worked for: Columbia Records

Biography

Johnny Cash was an American country singer and songwriter of the 20th century. He was born to poor cotton farmers in Kingsland, Arkansas. He worked countless hours in the cotton farm with his family.Became interested in music through hearing it through his old house's radio. Upon graduation High School, he enlisted in the Air Force. It was there he learned how to play guitar and began writing songs in his military service in Germany in the early 1950's. After his enlistment he settled in Memphis, Tennesee with his wife Vivian and sold appliance while in pursuit of a music career. Eventually, after Elvis' breakthrough he audtioned in Sun Records and landed on a successful deal.Through that he preformed several shows. In 1958, he left Sun Records for Columbia Records.He would go on many tours especially with the Carter family where he eventually fell in love with June Carter. He would later divcorce his wife Vivian and would take a while to propose to June.He continued his music career up until his late death in 2003.
